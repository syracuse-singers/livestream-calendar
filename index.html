Livestream Concerts and Song Sessions

A number of people have published Google Calendars for livestream concerts and song sessions. However, keeping track of multiple calendars can get unwieldy quickly. I am tracking these and then publishing them as a master calendar. The drawback to this system is that if I add a new calendar source, I have to remake the calendar and a new URL is created.
